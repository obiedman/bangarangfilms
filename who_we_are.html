WHO WE ARE

Bangarang Films is a leading Boston video production company founded by Kevin Briggs and JJ Rok in 2011 that specializes in professional HD video and multimedia content. We work in a range of media styles from live events to documentaries serving individual, commercial and corporate interests.

“Bangarang” is a reference to Steven Spielberg’s 1991 movie, “Hook”, where it serves as the Lost Boys’ battle cry. Bangarang has been a mantra of ours since college—it represents passion, belief, and a desire to always stay young. We try to keep the bangarang spirit alive in everything we produce.
