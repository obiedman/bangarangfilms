WHO WE ARE

Bangarang Films is a video production company founded by Kevin Briggs and JJ Rok in 2011. “Bangarang” is a reference to Steven Spielberg’s 1991 movie, “Hook”, where it serves as the Lost Boys’ cry of approval. Bangarang has been a mantra of ours since college—it represents passion, belief, and a desire to always stay young. We try to keep the bangarang spirit alive in everything we produce.
